Themes

Hackathon will be based on these domains.

E-Commerce

The ultimate goal of E-commerce is to provide goods and services on any electronic virtual platform via primarily the internet. The only focus of E-commerce is to achieve comsumer satisfaction and generate profits maintaining a stable growth of an existing or a new organisation. Create your own E-commerce platform and come up with innovative ideas to promote a business in unique ways.

Chatbots

A chatbot is a service powered by rules and sometimes Artifical Intelligence,that you interact with via a chat interface.With bots,the possibilities are endless. You can build anything unimaginable. It's potentially a huge business opportunity for anyone willing to jump headfirst and build something people want. Create your very own bot to come up with solutions to real-time scenarios!

App and Website Cloning

App and Website clone in a nutshell is creating a new website or App with improved design and functionality inspired from an existing website or App. If executed properly a website/App clone could be a game changer for your business in many ways. Create your own website/an application to overcome real time problems and improve an already existing functionality.


Home Automation

Home automation is a concept of smart home involving automation of lighting, heating, air conditioning, ventilation, security and various other home and office appliances which can be remotely controlled. Switches and sensors are connected to a central hub from which system is controlled with a user interface interacted via mobile, computer, web interface etc... using a dedicated software. Got the skills to automate things? Design your own home automation system in the most simple, unique and user friendly way.

Cyber Security

Cybersecurity is the body of technologies, processes and practices designed to protect networks, computers, programs and data from attack, damage or unauthorized access. Have an urge for protecting systems, network etc ? Come up with solutions to overcome real time problems.
